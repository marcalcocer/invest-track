Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 4, 2026

Who We Are

InvestTrack is an open-source personal finance application for tracking and managing investments, developed and maintained by independent contributors. The project is hosted at https://github.com/marcalcocer/invest-track.

What Data We Access

  • Google account basic profile (for authentication)
  • Google Sheets files you explicitly select for investment data storage and synchronization

How We Use Your Data

  • To read and write investment data to your selected Google Sheets
  • To display your investment information in the InvestTrack dashboard
  • To provide forecasting and analytics features

We do not share, sell, or use your data for advertising or any purpose unrelated to the app's core functionality.

Data Storage and Security

All investment data is stored in your own Google Sheets. No personal or investment data is stored on our servers. Authentication tokens are stored securely and are only used to access your data as needed for app functionality.

Third-Party Services

InvestTrack uses Google APIs to access your Google Sheets. All access is subject to Google's privacy policies and your explicit consent.

Your Rights and Choices

  • You can revoke InvestTrack's access to your Google account at any time via your Google account settings.
  • You may delete your investment data directly from your Google Sheets at any time.

Changes to This Policy

We will update this policy if our data practices change. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

Contact

For questions or concerns, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
