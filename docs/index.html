InvestTrack

Your personal finance web application for tracking and managing investments.

About InvestTrack

InvestTrack is an open-source web application that helps users track, manage, and forecast their investments. It provides a dashboard, detailed investment views, interactive charts, and Google Sheets integration for data storage and synchronization.

Functionality

  • Dashboard summary: total invested, value, benefit, profitability
  • Investment list and details
  • Interactive charts and performance graphs
  • CRUD operations for investments and entries
  • Forecasting with multiple scenarios
  • Google Sheets integration for secure data storage

Why does InvestTrack request access to your Google account?

InvestTrack uses your Google account solely to access and synchronize your investment data with your own Google Sheets. Your data is never shared with third parties, and all access is transparent and under your control. You can revoke access at any time from your Google account settings.
